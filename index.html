Welcome! I'm Asad Mehasi, a dedicated Data Scientist with a strong foundation in Mathematics and Numerical Analysis. My passion for automation and machine learning drives me to create innovative solutions that add value to businesses.

Learn about me

In addition to my extensive experience in R, Python, SAS, Spark, SQL, Power BI, and Tableau, I've contributed significantly to companies like Swinton Group (part of Atlanta Group), where I successfully integrated Python models into Radar via PMML and automated data preprocessing to streamline workflows.

At Ticker, I worked on pricing a new telematics insurance product specifically designed for Older Drivers aged 65+. This product addresses the challenge of higher premiums traditionally faced by this demographic. Although older drivers may have a slightly higher claim frequency, the claim severity tends to be lower. Our aim was to create a pricing strategy that does not penalize age alone, making insurance fairer while still managing risk effectively through telematics.

My current freelance work includes the PDF Table Extraction Toolkit, demonstrating my expertise in OCR, clustering (DBSCAN), and data preprocessing. This lightweight tool can extract tabular data from PDFs on machines without GPU support.

I am also exploring how modern Data Science tools like Bayesian A/B testing and DevOps pipelines can help bridge the gap in traditional insurance pricing. Historically dominated by actuarial teams, pricing is now evolving—data science can lead the way, and organizations that embrace this shift will stay competitive.
